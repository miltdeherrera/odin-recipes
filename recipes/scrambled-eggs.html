Scrambled Eggs

Delicious eggs that anyone would enjoy

These eggs have buttermilk in them. Boy, are they tasty!

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tbsp buttermilk
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Mix the eggs up in a bowl with the buttermilk, but don't mix them too much.
  2. Meanwhile, melt butter over medium-high flame.
  3. Once butter is melted and foam has subsided, pour eggs in and stir briskly until curd sets.
  4. When curd has set, dump pan of eggs onto plate and gorge.
